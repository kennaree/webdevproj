Our small business was founded by Makenna Rees, a woman who has a deep love for art and beauty and decided to chase her dreams of owning a flower shop.

Since 2018, we have grown tremendously. We have moved into our new location in Bountiful, Utah and have acquired an amazing team of strong women who love what they do everyday.
